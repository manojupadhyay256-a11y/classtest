Word Upload Template - Question Paper Format
=============================================
Copy and paste the formats below into your Microsoft Word document.
Keep ONE BLANK LINE between each question.

TYPE NAMES YOU CAN USE:
  mcq          -> Multiple Choice Question
  fill         -> Fill in the Blank / One Word Answer / Short Answer
  jumbled      -> Scrambled Word or Sentence
  match        -> Match the Following
  truefalse    -> True or False

---

1. Multiple Choice (MCQ)

Question: How many generations can computer languages be classified into?
Type: mcq
Marks: 1
Options:
A) Two
B) Four
C) Five
D) Six
Answer: C

---

2. Fill in the Blank / One Word Answer

Question: The brain of the computer is called the ______.
Type: fill
Marks: 1
Answer: CPU

---

3. True / False

Question: The Internet was invented by Tim Berners-Lee.
Type: truefalse
Marks: 1
Answer: true

---

4. Jumbled Word
(System automatically scrambles the letters and shows them as tiles)

Question: Unscramble the letters to form a device name.
Type: jumbled
Marks: 2
Answer: computer

---

5. Jumbled Sentence
(System automatically shuffles the words as tiles)

Question: Rearrange the words to form a correct sentence.
Type: jumbled
Marks: 2
Answer: The dog sat on the mat

---

6. Match the Following
(Write Left : Right pairs, one per line)

Question: Match the following countries with their capitals.
Type: match
Marks: 3
Answer:
India : New Delhi
France : Paris
Japan : Tokyo

---

QUICK TIPS:
* Always include: Question:, Type:, Marks:, Answer:
* For MCQ, also include Options: with A), B), C), D) on separate lines
* For Match, write each pair on its own line using colon (:) between them
* For Jumbled, just write the correct answer - the app scrambles it automatically
* For True/False, write:  Answer: true  OR  Answer: false
